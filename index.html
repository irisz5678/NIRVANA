Who We Are

Nirvana Memorial Garden (Singapore) was officially established in 2009. We are a subsidiary of Nirvana Asia Group, a leading and reputable funeral services group in the Asia-Pacific region. With a strong presence across multiple countries, we bring global expertise and a deep understanding of local cultures to Singapore.

Our Vision

To be a caring and responsible organization that touches the hearts of many, providing solace and support to families during their time of need.

Our Mission

To demonstrate the highest degree of professionalism, compassion, and integrity in all our services, ensuring that every moment is treated with the utmost respect and dignity.

Our Core Values: LIFE

  • L - Love: Care and respect to everyone, regardless of race, religion, or background.
  • I - Integrity: Honest and doing the right thing in all our dealings.
  • F - Focus: Delivering value to customers with unwavering dedication.
  • E - Empathy: Compassion to ourselves and others, sharing the pain and sorrow of families.
