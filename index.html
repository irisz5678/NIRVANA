Who We Are

Established in 2009, Nirvana Memorial Garden is part of Malaysian conglomerate Nirvana Asia Group, a leading provider of funeral and bereavement services in the Asia-Pacific region.

With years of experience and a strong presence across multiple countries, we bring professional, caring and respectful services to families in Singapore.
